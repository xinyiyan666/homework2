About the Nintendo game —— Animal Crossing

BASIC INFORMATION

Animal Crossing has become one of the most played games on Nintendo consoles. The latest installment was launched in March 2020 for Nintendo Switch and Switch lite users. In the same year, as the COVID-19 pandemic started and lockdowns came into effect, plenty of people resorted to video games as a source of fun. Animal Crossing: New Horizons was among the most popular games of 2020, and remains popular today. The game sold over 31 million copies worldwide, as per Video Games Chronicle.

WHY GAMERS LOVE IT?

The game offers adventures for players of all ages, and while players new and old are aware of common activities, such as fishing, decorating, and creating friendships, it's still possible to miss some exciting activities that gamers never knew exicted.

WHAT CAN YOU DO IN THIS GAME

While the game offers many things to explore, the core gameplay loop is simple. When you start, you’ll be given a basic dwelling and a loan from Tom Nook. You earn currency (known as bells) to pay off your loan, which in turn upgrades your dwelling. You’ll then be given a new loan to work off until you’ve got the biggest house in the village. But there are plenty of other things to do along the way. There’s a museum to fill with bugs, fossils, paintings, and more. You can collect shells, fruit, and seashells to sell for a profit. You can spend time socializing and sending letters or gifts to residents and real-life friends.
